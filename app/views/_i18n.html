How to use it

  • Include translation files for each of the desired locales, with the appropriate keys. The files should reside under [web oot context]/i18n (app/i18n for yeonam projects), and follow the below naming convention:
    • constants_[locale].json for constants.
    • messages_[locale].json for messages (allows for variable injection).
