How to use it

Include translation files for each of the desired locales, with the appropriate keys. The files should reside under [web oot context]/i18n (app/i18n for yeonam projects), and follow either of the below naming convention:

  • translations_[locale].json (default, no further configuration required).
  • constants_[locale].json.
  • messages_[locale].json.
